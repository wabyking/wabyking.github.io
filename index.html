Benyou Wang

@ Google Scholar      @ Blogs      @ Tencent
Benyou Wang is a NLP Research Engineer (titled with Research Associate in Tencent) in Tencent Shenzhen, China. (Tencent is one of two biggest companies in China with more than 40000 staffs, its market value had ever exceeded Facebook in 2017). Before joining Tencent in July. 2017, he has been a Research Assistant at Tianjin University since Feb. 2017. Benyou received his Master degree in Feb. 2017 from School of Computer Science and technology of Tianjin University, supervised by Professor Dawei Song and Doctor Peng Zhang . Before that, he received a B.Sc degree from the Huat in 2014. Benyou's research interests include (but not limited) information retrieval, natural language processing, machine learning and quantum mechanics. He is now working on many textual problems, models, algorithms and innovations. Particularly,
  • Deep Learning for NLP (e.g. Question Answering, Image caption)
  • Information Retrieval (e.g. Query Expansion, Recomendation System, Session Search)
  • Quantum inspired Models in Language (e.g. Quantum language model)
  • Adversarial Learning and Reinforce Learning
My work on question answering and query expansion has been part of many Tencent products and services, including Tencent Cloud, Tencent HR customer service. And some work about Q-zone short video stream Recommendation will be deployed latter.
